What is a personal injury attorney?

” What is a personal injury attorney? Well, personal injury attorneys are employed by consumers who have been involved in some misfortune or accident in which they’ve suffered bodily injury. Those accidents can be from all different types of situations:
  • Product defects where a product explodes or breaks causing injury to a party
  • Car accidents where somebody is injured as the result of negligent driving
  • Motorcycle accidents
  • A slip and fall or trip and fall case at the grocery store
  • Medical malpractice
Generally, personal injury firms take cases on a no-win no fee basis.“

Do I Have a Personal Injury Claim?
If you’ve been hurt through no fault of your own, you may have a case. Personal injury lawsuits attempt to recover financial damages from the negligent party to compensate the victim for their injuries. Although money can never completely make up for the pain and suffering that a severe injuries causes to the victim and their family, adequate compensation can relieve the financial distress that usually results from an unexpected tragedy. In other words, let the best San Diego Personal Injury Attorney help you stop worrying about paying the bills so that you can focus your energy on what’s really important, your recovery.
Personal injuries can be a result of the actions, the inaction or the negligence of one or more individuals or organizations. The most common causes include car accidents, medical malpractice, wrongful death and workplace accidents , but anytime you’re injured due to no fault of your own, it can fall under the legal definition of personal injury. When your claim is presented in a court of law, you must prove that the other party was both liable and negligent to recover. This means that they were legally required to exercise a certain level of care, they failed to do so and that you were injured or damaged in some way.

If you or someone you care about is injured due to someone else’s negligence, take these steps to protect your interests:
  • Do not wait to seek medical care in San Diego or outside San Diego. Delays can raise several questions including whether you were really hurt, the date of the injury or the actual source of the damage. Keep records of every doctor you see and how you feel on a daily basis.
  • Record the names of witnesses and other pertinent facts. If you’re able, create a file with all relevant information. Write down your own account of the accident as soon as you can just in case your memory of the event fades with time. Keep track of every medical expense and your lost time from work.
  • Consult with legal counsel based in San Diego as soon as possible. Only an experienced attorney can advise you of your rights and your responsibilities in a personal injury case. Most lawyers offer free consultations with no obligation. Never sign any legal documents or speak to anyone without your own representation.
